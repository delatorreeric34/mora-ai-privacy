Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 3, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 3, 2026

Introduction

MORA AI ("we," "us," or "our") operates an AI-powered voice ordering system for restaurants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when restaurants use our service to take phone orders on their behalf.

We are headquartered in San Diego, California. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at moraaisupport@gmail.com.

Information we collect

When a customer places an order through a restaurant using MORA AI, we collect the following information:

  • Name — provided by the customer during the order call
  • Phone number — the number from which the customer called
  • Order details — items ordered, quantities, modifiers, and order total
  • Call transcripts — text transcripts of the voice conversation used for order processing and quality assurance
  • Call metadata — call date, time, duration, and call identifier

We do not collect payment card information. Payment is handled at the restaurant upon pickup or delivery.

How we use your information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling restaurant orders
  • Transmitting order details to the restaurant's point-of-sale system
  • Resolving order disputes or customer service issues
  • Improving the accuracy and performance of our voice ordering system
  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

How we share your information

We share personal information only with the following parties, each of which acts as a service provider or data processor on our behalf:

  • Bland AI — voice call infrastructure and transcript processing (bland.ai)
  • Google Firebase / Google Cloud — secure data storage and backend infrastructure (firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
  • Clover / Fiserv — point-of-sale order submission for restaurants using Clover (clover.com/legal)
  • Square — point-of-sale order submission for restaurants using Square (squareup.com/legal/privacy)
  • The restaurant receiving the order — your name, phone number, and order details are shared with the restaurant to fulfill your order

We may also disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

Data retention

We retain personal information for the following periods:

  • Order data (name, phone, order details) — 12 months from the date of the order
  • Call transcripts and metadata — 90 days from the date of the call

After these periods, data is permanently deleted from our systems. Restaurants may retain their own copies of order data in their point-of-sale systems according to their own retention policies.

Your California privacy rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know — You may request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to delete — You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to correct — You may request that we correct inaccurate personal information we hold about you
  • Right to opt out of sale — We do not sell personal information. No opt-out is necessary.
  • Right to non-discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at moraaisupport@gmail.com. We will respond to verified requests within 45 days as required by California law. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted data storage via Google Firebase, access controls limiting who can view personal data, and secure HTTPS transmission for all data in transit.

No system is completely secure. If you have concerns about the security of your information, contact us at moraaisupport@gmail.com.

Children's privacy

Our service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us immediately at moraaisupport@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
